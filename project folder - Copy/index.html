This institute was founded with the twin objectives of preservation and progress:
preservation of the tradition, and progress in facing up to the challenges of the present age.
Enclosed within this vision was the specific objective of becoming a centre of excellence
in serving the needs of higher Islamic education in general, and, in particular, that of the Shāfiʿī madhhab
to which the people of the Cape largely subscribe
